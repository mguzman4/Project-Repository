Why hockey?

Why not hockey?! Other sports like basketball and soccer are exciting to watch, but they aren't hockey. Hockey players are passing a puck from one end of the ice to the other WHILE on ice skates. Not to mentions that there's a bit of action here and there between the guys especially with rivalries. Watching the guys play is so exciting and being in the area is exhilarating!
