Pizza Stickers

Here’s some simple math:

Pizza + Stickers = Pizza Stickers = Happy People

Pizza makes people happy. Stickers make people smile.

I love collecting stickers. One day I stumbled across these cute small orange stickers that say “Keep Calm and Eat Pizza” with a mini pizza on top. Ever since then, I often give them to my friends or put them on postcards that I send across the world.My goal is to spread these stickers all over the world in hopes to spread a love for pizza and smiles all around!
